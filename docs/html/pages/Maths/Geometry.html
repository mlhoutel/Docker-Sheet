Circles and Ellipse¶

Circle Equation

\((x-a)^2+(y-b)^2=r^2 \Leftrightarrow \sqrt{(x-a)^2+(y-b)^2}=r\)

Diameter

\(D=2 \times r\)

Circumference

\(C=2 \times \pi \times r\)

Area

\(A=\pi \times r^2\)

Ellipse Equation

\((\frac{(x-a)}{Hrad})^2+\frac{(y-b)}{Vrad})^2=1 \Leftrightarrow \sqrt{(\frac{(x-a)}{Hrad})^2+\frac{(y-b)}{Vrad})^2}=1\)
